Text-to-Speech Demo

A simple web app demonstrating how text sounds in different TTS voices.

Choose from any of the 57 voices in Amazon Polly (via Streamlabs API) or 48 voices in Google Translate. Polly voices can be useful for testing how Twitch.tv donations will sound as many streamers make use of these.

TTS Demo by Chris Phillips. Powered by Amazon Polly (via Streamlabs API) and Google Translate.
